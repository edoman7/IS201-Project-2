Burgess Owen's Internship
I got to intern with Utah's 4th District Congressman, Burgess Owens, on his reelection campaign. Here I got to assit with his campagin for the 2022 election. I witnessed first hand how important government officials are to the success of our everyday lives and learned how to communicate peacefully with opposing and non-opposing constituents.
