What I Have To Offer

Public Speaking
I pride myself in my talent of public speaking. I consider preparing speeches and talks for an audience as one my strong suits. It does not phase me to speak to a crowd, and thus I am always more than willing to take that responsibility from those who are not as comfortable.

Organization and Leaderhsip
I am a very organized person. I like to have my room, home, schedule, and phone lock screen organized. I also love helping others organize their lives, tasks, or things. It is more relaxing to me than anything else. I like to take the lead of group projects and make sure that they are organized in a way that will offer the best possible results. I am happy to work in a team and contribute, but I also find myself in the role of delegator quite often.

Communication With All Ages
I believe the best skill I can offer any company or organization is my ability to communicate with all ages. I have always had a knack for communicating with those who are older than me by large age gaps. I was always the oldest in my grade by a full year, so I also know how to communicate effectively with those who are younger than me. And I am always at ease with children and love learning from their unique and innocent perspectives. I consider my communication skills one of my greatest assests to any job asked of me.
