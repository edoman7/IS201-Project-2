BENSON BOONE

Recently, Benson Boone has been one of my favorite artists and his music is on repeat wherever I go.

Benson Boone is a singer and songwriter from Monroe, Washington. He discovered his talent when he particpated in the battle of bands with his friends in high school. He auditioned and made it onto American Idol in early 2021. He ultimatly left the show, but not without catching the attention of Americans and many other fans around the globe. His success started with his hit single "Ghosttown". Soon after he released 2 EPs. The first is titled "Walk Me Home" and includes 7 songs, the most popular being "In the Stars". His next EP came out in the fall of 2023. His most popular song from that EP was "Sugar Sweet". Over the course of the next 6 months he has released multiple other singles, before his first official debut album "Fireworks and Rollerblades" was released April 5, 2024. He is currently on tour with this album and hosts sold out shows all across the United States. His tour will expand worldwide this coming summer.
